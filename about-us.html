About Us

The IIPE JNTUK Student Chapter is a prestigious student-run organization established to bring together engineering students with a passion for production and mechanical engineering. Operating under the Department of Mechanical Engineering at the University College of Engineering, Kakinada (A), we are committed to fostering innovation, practical learning, and leadership skills among students.

Our chapter is part of the larger Indian Institute of Production Engineers, a national professional body that promotes the advancement of production engineering and related disciplines. Through this affiliation, we strive to create a platform for knowledge sharing, skill enhancement, and industry interaction.

Our Mission

To provide opportunities for students to gain exposure to the latest trends and technologies in production and mechanical engineering.
To bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application through workshops, industrial visits, guest lectures, and hands-on projects.
To foster a collaborative environment where students can network with industry professionals, alumni, and experts, thus preparing them for a successful career.

Our Vision

To become a leading student chapter that consistently promotes excellence in the field of production engineering.
To empower students by providing them with the tools, knowledge, and opportunities needed to become innovators and leaders in their respective fields.
